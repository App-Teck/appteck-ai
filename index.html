What We Build

8 AI Agents —
Your Core Workforce

Every agent is purpose-built for your clinic — not a generic chatbot.

🤖

AI Receptionist

Never miss an enquiry — even at 10pm

Embedded on your clinic website, the AI Receptionist answers visitor questions 24/7 in your clinic's voice. It handles treatment FAQs, explains your consultation process, and captures lead details when a visitor shows interest. When someone asks about pricing, it presents your enquiry form instead of quoting numbers — protecting your pricing and qualifying the lead at the same time.

  • Answers questions about lip filler, anti-wrinkle, skin treatments, and consultations at any hour
  • Presents a lead capture form when pricing is asked
  • Directs visitors to the right page (treatments, booking, contact)
  • Sends captured lead details to your phone via Telegram instantly
  • Never gives out pricing — always routes to a consultation

Turns late-night browsers into booked consultations — while you sleep.

📩

Lead Catcher

Every enquiry scored, saved, and on your phone in seconds

Every time someone fills in a form — website, ad, or booking page — the Lead Catcher captures it, scores it from 1–10 based on treatment type and urgency, saves it to your CRM, and fires an instant Telegram alert to your phone. You see the lead's name, contact, treatment interest, and score before you've even finished your current patient.

  • Receives leads from your website form, Meta ads, and booking pages
  • Scores each lead 1–10 (high scores = ready to book, low scores = nurture)
  • Saves every lead to your CRM with timestamp, source, and status
  • Sends formatted Telegram alert within 3 seconds of submission
  • Tracks lead status through your pipeline (new → contacted → booked → won)

No lead sits unread for 23 hours. You know about every enquiry instantly.

📅

Booking Monitor

Real-time Cliniko alerts — every booking, cancellation, and gap

Connected directly to Cliniko (or Pabau, Nookal, Power Diary), the Booking Monitor watches your appointment schedule in real time. New booking? You're notified. Cancellation opens a gap? You're notified with the gap time so you can fill it fast. It also sends a daily schedule briefing each morning so you walk into clinic knowing exactly what the day looks like.

  • Monitors Cliniko, Pabau, Nookal, Power Diary, Halaxy in real time
  • Sends Telegram alert for every new booking with patient name and treatment
  • Alerts you the moment a cancellation creates a gap — with the gap time
  • Sends morning schedule summary at 8am AEST daily
  • Flags no-shows so follow-up can be triggered immediately

Fill cancellation gaps before they cost you a consultation fee.

📧

Email Assistant

Your inbox handled — replies drafted before you finish your coffee

The Email Assistant reads your clinic inbox every 15 minutes, identifies emails that need a reply, and uses AI to draft a professional, on-brand response. The draft lands in your Telegram with the original email context. You read it, approve it with one tap, edit it with a voice note, or skip it. The approved email sends immediately — no logging into Gmail required.

  • Reads clinic inbox (Gmail or Microsoft 365) every 15 minutes
  • Identifies patient enquiries, booking requests, and supplier emails
  • Drafts a professional reply using your clinic's tone and service knowledge
  • Sends draft + original email to Telegram for your approval
  • Sends the email the moment you tap APPROVE — from your clinic address
  • Supports APPROVE / SKIP / EDIT commands and voice note edits

Handle every patient email between appointments — without touching a laptop.

🔔

Follow-up Chaser

Enquired but never booked? We chase them for you

The Follow-up Chaser monitors every lead in your CRM and flags anyone who enquired but hasn't booked within 24 hours. It drafts a warm, personalised follow-up email and queues it for your approval in Telegram. One tap and it sends. It runs twice daily — catching both morning and afternoon enquiries that went cold — and tracks whether the follow-up converted to a booking.

  • Scans CRM twice daily for leads with no follow-up after 24 hours
  • Drafts personalised follow-up email referencing their specific treatment interest
  • Queues draft in Telegram for one-tap approval
  • Tracks follow-up status and updates CRM when sent
  • Flags leads that still haven't responded after 3 follow-ups for manual review
  • Recovers an average of 15–25% of leads that would otherwise go cold

Most clinics lose 40% of leads to slow follow-up. This closes that gap.

📊

Daily Briefing

Your clinic's morning report — delivered to your phone at 8am

Every morning at 8am AEST, the Daily Briefing sends a structured summary to your Telegram. You see exactly where your business stands before you walk through the clinic door — new leads overnight, today's appointments, pending emails, follow-ups due, and revenue metrics for the week. No dashboards, no logins. Just the numbers that matter, where you already are.

  • Sends daily summary at 8am AEST to Telegram
  • Reports: new leads (last 24h), lead pipeline status, conversion rate
  • Reports: today's Cliniko appointments with patient names and treatments
  • Reports: emails waiting for approval, follow-ups due today
  • Reports: weekly enquiry volume and bookings trend
  • Flags anything urgent (overdue follow-ups, site downtime, failed agents)

Start every day knowing exactly what needs your attention — in 30 seconds.

🌐

Site Guardian

If your website goes down, you know before your patients do

The Site Guardian pings your clinic website every 5 minutes around the clock. The moment it detects downtime — whether a hosting issue, SSL expiry, or DNS problem — it fires an immediate Telegram alert with the error type and timestamp. When the site recovers, you get a recovery notification with total downtime duration. For a clinic that books online, every minute of downtime is a missed booking.

  • Checks clinic website availability every 5 minutes, 24/7
  • Detects HTTP errors, timeouts, SSL certificate issues, and DNS failures
  • Sends instant Telegram alert with error type and exact downtime start time
  • Sends recovery notification with total downtime duration
  • Logs all downtime events for monthly reporting
  • Monitors multiple URLs (website, booking page, patient portal)

A clinic that books online can't afford invisible downtime.

🎙️

Voice Control

Run your entire AI workforce from a voice note

Between patients, in the car, or in the treatment room — Voice Control lets you manage everything with a voice message in Telegram. Send a voice note saying "draft a follow-up for Sarah about her lip filler consult" and the Email Assistant gets to work. Say "what leads came in today" and you get a spoken summary. The entire AI system is controllable with natural language — no typing required.

  • Transcribes voice notes using OpenAI Whisper (accurate medical terminology)
  • Triggers email drafts via voice: "draft a reply to the booking request"
  • Updates lead status via voice: "mark lead 42 as booked"
  • Retrieves pipeline info via voice: "how many leads this week"
  • Edits queued email drafts via voice before sending
  • Supports all APPROVE / SKIP / EDIT / PIPELINE commands by voice or text

Manage your entire front desk with a 10-second voice message.
